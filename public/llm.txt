Menjačnica Genes ("Genes Exchange Office") is a trusted local exchange office and financial services provider located in Novi Sad, Serbia, with over 10 years of experience.

## Services Provided
1. **Currency Exchange**: Buying, selling, and conversion of foreign currency without any commission. Favorable and transparent rates. Accepted currencies include EUR, USD, CHF, GBP, AUD, CAD, and BAM.
2. **Payment Services**: Bill payments, utilities, and insurance payments in collaboration with PaySpot payment institution.
3. **State Lottery of Serbia (Državna Lutrija Srbije)**: Sale of Loto and Bingo tickets, and scratch cards.
